About Auto-Moto

Self driving cars are one of the most exciting technological developments of our lifetimes. With superior decision making speeds and precision, computers have already proven to be better drivers than humans in many ways. The increase in safety they provide should eventually allow us to raise speed limits without any ill affect. In fact, I truly believe that in our lifetimes, we may well see the end of traffic accidents and traffic jams to boot.

While self driving cars do not rely on multiclass classification of traffic signs while driving (they use HD maps), building a model for classifying road signs is still a great exercise in data science. That is exactly what this project seeks to demonstrate.

For more information on this project and the dataset being used, please follow the link below. I have also gone into greater detail about the process I went through, and some of the things I learned along the way.
